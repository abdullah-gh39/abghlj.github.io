About Me

Hi, I'm Abdullah Majed Al Ghamdi, a 18-year-old full-stack developer from Saudi Arabia. I'm passionate about building and creating innovative web applications. I have a strong foundation in both front-end and back-end development, and I'm always looking for new challenges and opportunities to learn and grow.

I started my programming journey at the age of 15, when I was first introduced to HTML and CSS. I was immediately fascinated by the ability to create and design websites from scratch. I quickly learned the basics of these languages, and I began to experiment with different designs and layouts.

After I had a good understanding of HTML and CSS, I moved on to learning JavaScript. JavaScript is a programming language that allows you to add interactivity to your web pages. I was amazed by the possibilities that JavaScript offered, and I spent countless hours learning the language.

Once I had a good understanding of JavaScript, I began to learn about back-end development. Back-end development is the process of building the server-side of a web application. I learned about different back-end programming languages, such as Python and Node.js, and I also learned about different back-end frameworks, such as Django and Express.js.

I am a highly motivated and results-oriented individual. I am always willing to go the extra mile to get the job done. I am also a team player and I am always willing to help others. I am confident that I have the skills and the drive to be a successful full-stack developer.
